# Landing Page Example

Prompt:

```text
Build a premium landing page with a clear offer, restrained hero entrance, scroll-led product proof, accessible CTAs, and reduced-motion support. Use GSAP ScrollTrigger only for the product walkthrough and Motion for small component states.
```

Acceptance criteria:

- The offer is clear in the first viewport.
- The hero has one signature motion idea.
- Product proof appears before abstract claims.
- Scroll effects support the story.
- The page remains excellent with reduced motion.
