# Animation Principles

Premium motion is calm, useful, and specific. It clarifies hierarchy, causality, attention, and state.

## Timing

- Hover: 120-220ms.
- Tap: 80-160ms.
- Modal: 180-320ms.
- Section reveal: 450-900ms.
- Hero entrance: 600-1200ms.
- Page transition: 180-450ms.

## Easing

Use `power3.out`, `power4.out`, `expo.out`, or equivalent cubic curves. Avoid bounce and elastic for serious products unless brand tone justifies them.

## Sequencing

Reveal containers, then primary message, then proof, then CTA, then secondary detail. Stagger only inside meaningful groups.

## Reduced Motion

Reduced motion keeps quality but removes movement. Replace large movement, parallax, and scroll-linked transforms with instant state or short opacity transitions.
